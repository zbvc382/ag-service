about a&g appliances

A&G Appliances is an established, professional, domestic appliance repair company. Our appliance repair engineers are fully trained and competent in repairing electric oven/cookers, dishwashers, washing machines, dryers, and fridge/freezers. We cover most of London, working in postcodes within the M25 apart from a few: CR, HA, UB, KT, TW, DA.

  • Family business.
  • Many happy, repeat customers.
  • Your domestic appliance repair guaranteed.
  • Our engineers use the latest technologies and manufacturer’s data for a more reliable and faster repair.

if you choose us

Here's what you get if you choose A&G Appliances for your appliance repair.

1.

simple pricing structure

  • A fixed diagnostic charge of £35 which covers the time spent identifying the cause of the fault.
  • A repair charge payable only after your appliance is repaired and confirmed as working.
  • No service fee is charged if the engineer is unable to identify the fault.
  • If new parts are required, we’ll give you a fixed quote for these before proceeding with the repair.

2.

easy way to pay

To make things easy, we accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and cash. Our commercial customers can be invoiced and choose to pay by cheque, bank transfer or card.

3.

guaranteed for peace of mind

We guarantee all our work and parts fitted for six months. This means if the appliance develops the same fault, we will fix it for you, free of charge.
