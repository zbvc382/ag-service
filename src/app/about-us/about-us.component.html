about a&g appliances

A&G Appliances is an established, professional, domestic appliance repair company. Our appliance repair engineers are fully trained and competent in repairing electric oven/cookers, dishwashers, washing machines, dryers, and fridge/freezers. We cover most of London, working in postcodes within the M25 apart from a few: CR, HA, UB, KT, TW, DA.

how we work

Here’s what happens if you choose A&G Appliance Repairs for your appliance repair:

1.

Call out

We can make an appointment for you to diagnose what’s wrong with your machine. Our appliance repair engineer are experienced and will be able to explain what’s wrong, and what will be required to fix it. If you decide not to proceed with the repair, or if your machine is beyond repair, there is a charge for the engineer to travel to you and diagnose a problem.

2.

Fixed price repairs

So that you can have an accurate idea of how much a repair is likely to cost, we charge a fixed labour charge of £50 (includes call out) plus parts. (£50 for integrated washer and £55 for a dishwasher). Prices are plus VAT. Two-thirds of our repairs are completed without the need for new parts, but if new parts are required, we’ll give you a fixed quote for these before proceeding with the repair. The average cost of parts, if they are required, is about £15-30, but this does vary depending on what’s wrong with your machine.

3.

Simple payments

To make things easy, we accept all major credit and debit cards using over the phone card processing, as well as cash or cheque (£2 card processing fee applies). Subject to credit, our commercial customers can be invoiced and choose to pay by cheque, bank transfer or card.

4.

Guaranteed for peace of mind

It unlikely, but we guarantee all our work and parts fitted for six months. This means if the appliance develops the same fault, we’d fix it for you, free of charge.
