about a&g appliances

A&G Appliances is an established, professional, domestic appliance repair company. Our appliance repair engineers are fully trained and competent in repairing electric oven/cookers, dishwashers, washing machines, dryers, and fridge/freezers. if you choose us

Here's what you get if you choose A&G Appliances for your appliance repair.

1.

simple pricing structure

  • A fixed diagnostic charge of £35 which covers the time spent identifying the cause of the fault.
  • A repair charge that is payable after your appliance has been repaired and is confirmed to work.
  • No service fee is charged if the engineer is unable to identify the fault.
  • If new parts are required, we’ll give you a fixed quote for these before proceeding with the repair.
  • No extra charge for subsequent visits if new parts need to be ordered.
  • We provide 50% discount for each additional appliance needed to repair on the first visit.

2.

easy way to pay

To make things easy, we accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and cash. Our commercial customers can be invoiced and can pay by cheque, bank transfer or card.

3.

guaranteed for peace of mind

We guarantee all our work and parts fitted for six months. This means if the appliance develops the same fault, we will fix it for you, free of charge.
