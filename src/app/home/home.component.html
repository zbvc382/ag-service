June 15th, 2019

In quite a few instances we come across a problem where a person has an oven on automatic and they can’t use it.

For the oven to work on the automatic, the timer needs to be set. It’s best if the oven is in manual mode. Read your oven manual first before doing anything else.

If you don’t have a manual, try doing this: Try holding the ‘bell’ button down at the same time as you press the ‘pan x’ button.

From our experience, the most common problem with the electric ovens is the failure of the heating element.

In these instances, the heating element needs replacing, the average cost of the element is £30-£35 or more.

Our engineers carry heating elements with them and can repair the oven there and then. Recently we had a job where the heating element was fine, but the oven kept tripping the electricity in the house.
